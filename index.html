Key Features

Immutable Records

Tamper-proof blockchain records ensure complete data integrity throughout the entire supply chain.

Real-time Tracking

Monitor pharmaceutical products from manufacturing to patient delivery with real-time location updates.

Environmental Monitoring

IoT sensors track temperature, humidity, and other vital parameters to ensure drug integrity during transit.

Instant Verification

QR code scanning allows healthcare providers and patients to verify drug authenticity in seconds.

Automated Alerts

Receive instant notifications for anomalies or environmental condition violations during transport.

Smart Contracts

Automate ownership transfers and enforce compliance with predefined conditions through smart contracts.

How It Works

1

Product Registration

Manufacturers register drug details on the blockchain, creating an immutable entry with batch information, expiry date, and unique identifiers.

2

IoT Integration

IoT sensors attached to drug packages continuously monitor environmental conditions and feed data to the blockchain.

3

Chain of Custody

Every ownership transfer is securely recorded through smart contracts, creating a complete chain of custody from manufacturer to patient.

4

Verification

Healthcare providers and patients can scan QR codes to instantly verify product authenticity and access complete history.

Benefits

Patient Safety
